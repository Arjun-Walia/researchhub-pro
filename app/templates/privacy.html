Privacy policy

How we collect, process, and safeguard your research data across the ResearchHub Pro platform.

Contact privacy team View terms

1. Overview

ResearchHub Pro is committed to protecting customer, researcher, and stakeholder data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the safeguards we put in place to keep it secure.

2. Information we collect

  • Account data: Name, email, organization, and authentication details.
  • Usage data: Search queries, projects, collections, audit logs, and feature telemetry.
  • Support records: Communications with our support or success teams, including attachments.
  • Integration metadata: API keys or workspace identifiers you choose to connect.

3. How we use information

  • Deliver and maintain the ResearchHub Pro service.
  • Personalize dashboards, recommendations, and usage insights.
  • Provide customer support, security monitoring, and incident response.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce platform policies.

4. Data retention

We retain account and workspace data for the duration of your subscription, unless you request deletion earlier. Upon termination, we purge customer data within 30 days, except where legal or regulatory retention requirements apply.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may process data with trusted sub-processors supporting hosting, analytics, or communication services. Each provider signs data protection agreements and meets our security requirements.

6. International transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions where ResearchHub Pro or its partners operate. We use Standard Contractual Clauses and regional data residency controls where required.

7. Security safeguards

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest.
  • Role-based access control with audit logging.
  • Regular penetration testing and third-party security assessments.
  • Continuous monitoring for anomalous activity.

8. Your rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete personal data associated with your account. Workspace administrators can request workspace-wide exports or removals by contacting privacy@researchhub.pro.

9. Contact

For privacy inquiries, data requests, or to report security concerns, email privacy@researchhub.pro. We respond within two business days.
